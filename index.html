What is Asano?

Asano is an autonomous boat that aims to collect water samples from freshwater lakes in order to aid with water quality testing.
Asano would provide great assistance for one of the most important steps in water testing. It would collect and deliver uncontaminated water samples, making water testing a simpler process.

Why is it needed?

As water scarcity as well as water pollution increases, there has been an increase in the demand to reduce water pollution and improve the quality of water. For these reasons, there is a continuous demand to test water quality.

Target Market

Asano will help scientists and researchers in faster analysis of water quality which in turn would lead to faster solutions for improving the quality of water.
